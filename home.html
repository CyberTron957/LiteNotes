End-to-End Encryption

Your notes are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before being stored. Only you can decrypt them with your password.

Offline Support

Create and edit notes even when offline. Changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

Customizable UI

Multiple themes, fonts, and background patterns to match your style and improve readability.

Real-time Saving

Your notes are automatically saved as you type, with encrypted cloud backup when signed in.

Mobile Responsive

Fully optimized for mobile viewing. Take your notes anywhere on any device.

Cloud Sync & Merge

Local notes are automatically merged with your cloud notes when you sign in.
